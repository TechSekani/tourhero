Elegushi Beach,
Lekki

Elegushi beach is a delight to fun-seekers, as it offers a variety of activities, especially during festive periods.However, with the Elegushi beach, one is bound to have fun no matter the size of the pocket. Entry to the beach is N1, 000 per person and N200 for the car park. So, making a decision on how much to spend at the beach is relative as it is basically hinged on how long one would be staying, preferences and intentions.

Elegushi offers a mix of music, food, beautiful scenery of water. It is a must visit place for any tourist for its white sand beach ripping waves that lap the shoreline. It provides the ultimate relaxation for any.
