Ibeshe Beach,
Satellite Town

Ibeshe Private Beach Is A Beautiful And Safe Beach. An Island On Its Own, Modern Living And Leisure Facilities Have Been Built In It To Accommodate Fun Seekers Who Come To Enjoy A Serene Environment, Play Water Sports, Enjoy The Magnificent Scenery, And Watch The Tropical Sunsets. Tourists can enjoy continental dishes. The customer service offered is top-notch.

The beach itself is a must-see as it is the longest beach in Africa. The beach has a bridge that was built by the federal government. With its beautiful coastline, Ibeno provides endless natural facilities for tourists, such as; Boat Riding, Canoeing, Water sports, Volleyball, Beach soccer, Archery, Sunbathing, etc. The best time to visit there is during the dry season, from November to April. Preferably during the weekend, public holidays, or during festive periods.
